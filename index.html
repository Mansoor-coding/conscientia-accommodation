Conscientia, the annual Astronomy and Technology festival of the Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST) in Thiruvananthapuram, has been a beacon for science enthusiasts since its inception. Over the years, it has hosted numerous events and workshops, attracting participants from various parts of the country. The festival has featured esteemed speakers, including the late Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, and has showcased advancements in space technology and engineering through technical expos and hands-on workshops. The vibrant atmosphere and the convergence of ideas from diverse scientific spheres make Conscientia a unique platform for budding scientists and engineers.

Important Information

Protocol & Guidelines

To ensure a safe, secure, and productive environment for all participants, please adhere to the following protocols during your stay.

Check-in/Check-out: System check-in begins at 14:00. Please be prepared to vacate your unit by 11:00 on the day of departure. A valid government-issued photo ID or Tech-Pass is required for verification.

Unit Integrity: To protect the facility's life-support and network integrity, smoking and unauthorized hardware modifications are strictly prohibited within the habitation units.

Network & Noise Discipline: Please respect your fellow innovators. Observe quiet hours from 23:00 to 06:00. Malicious network activity is grounds for immediate disconnection and removal from the premises.

How to Reach
Transportation Guide

Transit Protocols

The IIST campus nexus is well-connected to all major city hubs. Below are the recommended transit options.

From Trivandrum International Airport (TRV): A 15 km journey, typically taking 30 minutes via a registered taxi service or ride-sharing app.

From Trivandrum Central Railway Station (TVC): Located 12 km from campus. Expect a 25-minute ride via auto-rickshaw or taxi.

Public Transit: The most economical option. Look for city buses on routes 7B or C-12 from the main bus depot (Thampanoor). These will drop you at Valiamala Junction, a short walk from the campus gate.
